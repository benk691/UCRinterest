Getting Started

Welcome and thank you for using UCRinterest!

To get started, navigate to the top-right of the page and click on the "Register" button.

Alternatively, clicking here will bring you back to the home page.

Registration

When you register, you will be brought to this form.

Registration is an easy process. To start, pick a username of your desire.

Pick a password. Your password should be anywhere from 3 to 50 characters long.

Next, fill in your first and last name.

Remember to add your email too so we can contact you.

Now add in your gender and birthday.

Lastly, enter a fancy description of yourself and press "Register"!

If you see this message, your username has been taken. Select another.

Congratulations on making a profile on UCRinterest!

Logging In and Out

Welcome and thank you for using UCRinterest!

To get started, navigate to the top-right of the page and click on the "Register" button.

You should reach the login page automatically after submitting your registration form.

You can always click on the "Login" button at the top-right at any time.

This will be the login form you'll see.

Just enter your username.

...Along with your password and press "Login".

Once you're logged in, you can log out at any moment as well.

User Profile

After you have logged in, you will be brought to the home page.

Click on your username to access your user profile.

You will then brought to your user profile.

This is a sample of what you may see.

Adding Photos

As a registered user, you can add photos now!

Just click here.

You will then be brought to this form.

Just add in a title. Any title that suits your fancy.

Then, browse your computer for the image file to upload.

Now add a dashing description and just press "Post"!

You will then be taken to the home page where you'll see the image you just uploaded.

Viewing Photos

You can view pictures as well.

Just click on it and you'll see a larger version.

About/Help/Team

Clicking the "About" button will bring you to our About page.

Clicking the "Help" button will bring you this help page.

Clicking the "Team" button will show you the people who made this possible.
